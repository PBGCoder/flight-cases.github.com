CUSTOM FLIGHT CASES
“Shree Ganesh Engineering Works " have been involved in the design and manufacture of custom built flightcases in the AV/ Broadcasting, Sound + Lighting and Electronic industries for over 10 years. The experience gained shines through in our efficient and competitive service Custom Flightcases designed for all types of application. Custom Built Flightcases are made to order in a range of colours and finishes using laminated birch plywood or ultra lightweight Plywood Aluminium extrusions and hardware are then added using steel bifurcated rivets wherever possible. Bifurcated rivets' holding power is much greater than the standard aluminium pop-rivets used by most manufacturers. This ensures the protection of what ever is being carried inside.
STACKABLE CASES
In order to meet the needs of industrials, our research department has developed a new generation of Palletizable Open Road containers. They have been studied to respect the INDIAN standards of transport while guaranteeing a maximum protection of the material. They can be used as shuttle crates, palletized crates or transport trunks. The materials used as well as the manufacturing techniques used emphasize robustness, reliability and endurance.

This case has many advantages. They are: - Reusable, - Robust, - Functional, - Economic, - Ergonomic, - Ideal for transport and storage, - Stackable / palletizable on Euro pallet, - Stackable Product specifications: - Useful interior dimensions: Custom - 9 mm birch plywood, black crossover foam - 2 recessed flip handles - 2 plates for stacking - Inserts M8 for mounting wheels

ROLLING FLIGHT CASES
We supply rolling flight cases, usually from stock, in 4 standard sizes. Features include hinged lid, the option of partitions, which can slide in lengthways or widthways to suit, positioned at 30mm intervals. Silk screen printing of logos available if required. Shree Ganesh Engineering Works manufactures its range of rolling flight cases from 9mm black plywood, with aluminium extrusions and steel protective corners. Hardware includes 75mm recessed corner castors, recessed telescopic handle and padlocking butterfly catches.
